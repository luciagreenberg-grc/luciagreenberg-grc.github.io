About us

Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, Al Dente is a celebration of classic Italian cooking, time-honored traditions, and the simple joy of a perfectly cooked plate of pasta. Family-owned and proudly old-fashioned, we believe great food begins with great craft—fresh ingredients, slow simmering, and recipes passed down through generations.

Since opening our doors, Al Dente has become a cozy gathering place for locals, visitors, and anyone who appreciates the comforting flavors of Italy. Our kitchen stays true to the classics: handmade pastas, rich sauces made daily, and the unmistakable aroma of garlic, basil, and simmering tomatoes that fills the dining room.

Here, meals are unrushed, conversations linger, and every dish is cooked “just right”—true to our name. Whether you’re joining us for a hearty plate of spaghetti and meatballs, a glass of wine with friends, or your new favorite Italian comfort dish, we’re honored to share our table with you.

At Al Dente, tradition is our recipe—and everyone is family.
